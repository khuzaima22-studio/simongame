Welcome to the Simon Game!

How to play:

  • The game will show you a sequence of flashing colors. Memorize the order.
  • Click the buttons in the same order as the sequence.
  • If you get it right, the sequence will grow longer. Keep playing as long as you can!
  • If you make a mistake, the game is over. Press "Start" to try again.

Good luck and have fun!
